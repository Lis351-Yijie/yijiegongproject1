Lactic Acid Bacterium

Abbreviated as "LAB"

Characteristics:

  1. Able to ferment sugars into lactic acid, can product fermented foods and beverages like yogurt, cheese, and sourdough bread.
  2. Gram-positive cells
  3. Probiotic, contributing to gut health by enhancing the intestinal microbiota and boosting the immune system.
  4. Exhibit a high tolerance to acidic conditions
  5. produce bacteriocins
